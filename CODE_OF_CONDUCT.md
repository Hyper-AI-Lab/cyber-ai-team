# Code of Conduct

Cyber AI Team welcomes contributors who want to build serious open-source infrastructure for AI-operated companies.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, but verify technical claims with evidence.
- Keep discussions focused on the project, users, safety, reliability, and maintainability.
- Be clear when something is experimental, risky, or unverified.
- Respect privacy and never post secrets or sensitive operational data.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private credentials, personal data, or exploit details.
- Encouraging unsafe autonomous behavior without approval, audit, and rollback controls.
- Repeatedly ignoring maintainer guidance on safety or project scope.

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or restrict participation to keep the project healthy.
